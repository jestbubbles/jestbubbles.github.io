Voice Acting

Jess Floam is an enthusiastic and passionate livestream musician, vocalist, and voice actor. Studied linguistics and phonetics in university, with focus in English, French, Spanish, and Russian. Particular interest in voicing US, UK, Russian, French accents as well as female characters from child to elder.
Send me a message with a description and your budget and I would love to bring your characters to life through voice.
