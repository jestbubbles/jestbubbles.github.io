Dorm Room Cat Girl

My first full-length audiobook narration, The Dorm Room Cat Girl, an anime-inspired novel by CS James.

In a Northern Tokyo boarding school, a stray cat that had used up all her nine lives is given a 10th chance at life as a cat-girl by a magical cat-god. The cat-god's magic is pretty weak, however, so in order to keep her 10th life, cat-girl has to collect love-magic from an unassuming high school boy. Sounds easy enough, right? Well...

Join our friends at the elite boarding school Kasumi Academy as they all try to piece together what it really means to be human.
