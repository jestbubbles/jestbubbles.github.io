Captain Jest, at yer service

Jess go with the Floam, narrator and all of the characters of the anime-inspired audiobook The Dorm Room Cat Girl. 
Jess is a music and gaming broadcaster, voice actor, 
musician, vocalist, cosplayer, and adventurer.
When she's not making noise, Jess loves to explore the outdoors with her sidekick Tikka the Cat. They hike mountains and camp in rainforests, kayak brackish waters and floated along in the ocean living on a sailboat. And while Tikka stays on board, Jess dives deep as an Advanced Open Water scuba diver. She is a siren singing ballads of no troubles, jest bubbles; Captain Jest of the mighty vessel the JestExpress, where she sails with her crewmates on waves of good vibration.
When she's not adventuring or at sea, Jess likes to spend her time rehabilitating orphan kittens. Some of the cuties cared for include Siegfried and Roy, Tom Sawyer, Huckleberry Finn, Boots, and the Hobbitses: Frodo, Sam, Pippin, Merry, and little Rosie.

The ever-exciting tales of two brave heroines:

Tikka the Cat and Jess the Human

as they journey through this grand adventure called life

From serenading local wildlife to center stage at live rock band (and Rockband) karaoke, I have always loved singing and performing music.
By my side, a Luna High Tide Koa concert soprano ukulele. Sometimes Tikka joins me with tamborine.
