Captain Jest, at yer service

Jess go with the Floam, the voice of the anime-inspired audiobook The Dorm Room Cat Girl. 
Jess is a Ukrainian-American voice actor, musician, vocalist,
music and gaming broadcaster, cosplayer, and adventurer.
When she's not making noise, Jess loves to explore the outdoors with her sidekick Tikka the Cat. They hike mountains and camp in rainforests, kayak brackish waters and float along in the ocean. 
When she's not adventuring or at sea, Jess likes to spend her time rehabilitating orphan kittens. Some of the cuties cared for include Siegfried and Roy, Tom Sawyer, Huckleberry Finn, Boots, and the Hobbitses: Frodo, Sam, Pippin, Merry, and little Rosie.


The ever-exciting tales of two brave heroines:

Tikka the Cat and Jess the Human

as they journey through this grand adventure called life

From serenading local wildlife to center stage, I have always loved singing and performing music.
By my side, a Luna High Tide Koa concert soprano ukulele. Sometimes Tikka joins me with tambourine.
